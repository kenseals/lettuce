# Churn risk: workflow breakage

Source: support email
From: Devon Lee, Customer Success at BrightCart
To: support@northstar.example
Subject: We may pause the rollout

Devon reports that BrightCart's weekly planning workflow broke after Northstar routed a customer escalation into the product backlog without human review.

The team expected Northstar to draft a suggested update, not mutate the shared planning board automatically. Devon says two managers lost trust because the agent looked like it was acting outside its authority.

Customer quote: "The output was not terrible, but the autonomy boundary was wrong. We need review before action."

Risk: BrightCart may pause rollout unless Northstar adds a visible review/approve step and a clear audit log.
