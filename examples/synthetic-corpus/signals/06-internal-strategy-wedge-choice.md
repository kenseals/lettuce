# Internal strategy: wedge choice

Source: internal strategy note
Author: Maya, Northstar operator

Northstar is choosing between three wedges:

1. Support-team context memory
2. Sales-call follow-up routing
3. Internal product decision brain

The strongest pull is support-team context memory because customers already complain when agents forget rollout constraints, escalation policies, and account-specific rules.

The weakest wedge is generic internal product decision brain. It is interesting, but less tied to urgent customer pain.

Decision proposal: focus the demo on support leaders who need reviewed, durable operating context for agents working across customer queues.
