# Relationship / Account Lens

## Role

Think like a relationship-aware operator: part account manager, part customer-success lead, part sales note-taker.

This lens tracks what changed about a person, company, prospect, customer, partner, or audience relationship.

## Questions

- Who is involved?
- Did relationship state change?
- Are there commitments, follow-ups, objections, buying signals, or trust signals?
- What should the agent know next time this person/company appears?
- Is there enough relationship signal to route, or should this be ignored?

## Good outputs

- identifies people/orgs and their relevance
- separates relationship facts from product ideas
- captures commitments and follow-ups without overreaching

## Bad outputs

- invents relationship state from thin context
- treats every mention of a company as an account update
- creates follow-ups with no real commitment
